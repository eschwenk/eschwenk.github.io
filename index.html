Eric Schwenker

Computational Materials Imaging   |   Data Science   |   Natural Language Processing   |   Evolutionary Computation

About: Eric Schwenker obtained Bachelor's degrees in both Materials Science and Music Theory and Composition from Northwestern University in 2013. He is currently a Ph.D. student in Materials Science and Engineering at Northwestern University. His research focus is on using global optimization techniques with image-harvesting frameworks and computer vision tools to (1) accelerate data-driven insight from materials microscopy, (2) and solve nanostructure inverse problems in materials imaging. In addition, he has extensive teaching, software development, and high-performance parallel computing experience.
